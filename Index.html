Story

Ever since introductory classes in high school, I have been hooked on computer science and more specifically computer programming. I continued this interest by enrolling in Niagara Colleges 3 year Computer Programmer Analyst program which I graduated from in 2018. I am currently employed full time as an Application Developer at Western I.T. Group.

My current interests are split between ASP.NET web application development and general I.T. infrastructure. I have been spending a lot of time at home testing and setting up various I.T. systems such as Proxmox Virtualization, PfSense, NAS, SAN, and Windows servers. Since I spend so much time programming during the day I tend to prefer learning different things at home that may not be strictly related to just programming.

I am always looking for new projects to work on and ways to challenge myself.
