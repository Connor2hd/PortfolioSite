Story

Ever since introductory classes in high school, I have been hooked on computer science and more specifically computer programming. I am currently attending Niagara College to further my education though I also spend a vast amount of time outside of the classroom learning and developing.

My current interests are split between ASP.NET web application development and general I.T. infrastructure. I have been spending a lot of time at home testing and setting up various I.T. systems such as Proxmox Virtualization, PfSense, NAS, SAN, and Windows servers.

I am always looking for new projects to work on and ways to challenge myself.
